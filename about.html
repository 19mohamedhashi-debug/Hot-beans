About Hot Beans Web

Learn more about who we are, what we do, and why we train the next generation of developers.

Who We Are

Hot Beans Web is a modern and forward‑thinking web development company based in the UK. We specialise in creating responsive, user‑friendly websites for businesses of all sizes. Our team is made up of passionate developers, designers, and digital creators who work together to deliver high‑quality digital solutions.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to build beautiful, functional websites while supporting new talent entering the tech industry. We believe in giving trainee developers real opportunities to learn, grow, and work on live projects that build their confidence and skills.

Why We Train Developers

The tech industry is growing rapidly, and we want to help shape the next generation of developers. Our trainee programme gives beginners hands‑on experience with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern web tools. Many of our trainees go on to become full‑time developers at Hot Beans Web or other companies.
