Job Specifications

Everything you need to know before applying to Hot Beans Web.

Role Overview

Hot Beans Web is looking for motivated junior developers who want to grow their skills and work on real client projects. You will work with experienced developers and learn industry‑standard tools and techniques.

Required Skills

  • Basic understanding of HTML and CSS
  • Some experience with JavaScript
  • Good communication skills
  • Ability to work in a team
  • Willingness to learn new technologies

Preferred Qualifications

  • BTEC Level 3 IT
  • A‑Level Computer Science
  • Online coding certificates
  • Portfolio of small projects (optional)

What We Offer

  • Hands‑on training with real projects
  • Support from senior developers
  • Friendly and supportive environment
  • Opportunities for full‑time employment
  • Access to online learning resources
